Product Information

Our chairs are made from high-grade polypropylene plastic with specialized additives for enhanced durability and weather resistance. Key material features include:

  • 75% post-consumer recycled plastic content
  • UV stabilizers to prevent fading and cracking
  • Impact modifiers for superior strength
  • Antimicrobial additives for hygiene
  • Colorfast pigments that won't fade

All materials are certified safe and meet international quality standards.

Yes! Most of our chairs are designed for both indoor and outdoor use. Our outdoor-rated models feature:

  • UV-resistant materials that won't fade in sunlight
  • Weather-resistant construction
  • Drainage holes to prevent water accumulation
  • Temperature resistance from -20°F to 140°F
  • Salt air and humidity resistance
Recommended for outdoor use: AquaStack Outdoor, EcoComfort Patio, EventStack series, and CafeComfort Lounge chairs.

Weight capacities vary by model:

  • EventStack Basic: 250 lbs
  • AquaStack Outdoor: 300 lbs
  • EventStack Premium: 350 lbs
  • ProMeet Conference: 350 lbs
  • FlexiWork Task: 300 lbs
  • EcoComfort Patio: 325 lbs
  • CafeComfort Lounge: 275 lbs
  • ModernCurve Accent: 250 lbs

All chairs are tested to 150% of their rated capacity for safety.

Consider these factors when selecting chairs:

  • Use Environment: Indoor, outdoor, or both
  • Frequency of Use: Daily, occasional, or event-based
  • Storage Needs: Stackable options save space
  • Comfort Level: Basic seating vs. ergonomic support
  • Style Preference: Professional, casual, or designer

Our sales team can help you choose the perfect chairs for your specific needs. Contact us at sales@flexiseatspro.com or call (555) 123-2448.
