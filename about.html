Manufacturing Excellence

Our advanced manufacturing facility utilizes state-of-the-art injection molding technology and automated quality control systems. We've invested heavily in precision equipment that ensures consistent quality across every product line.

Our proprietary plastic compound formula includes UV stabilizers and impact modifiers that provide superior durability and weather resistance. Each chair is designed using computer-aided engineering to optimize strength-to-weight ratios and ergonomic comfort.

We maintain strict quality control protocols, with every batch tested for load capacity, UV resistance, and dimensional accuracy. Our ISO 9001 certified processes ensure consistent quality that meets international standards.

Environmental Responsibility

At FlexiSeats Pro, we believe that quality furniture shouldn't come at the expense of our planet. Our commitment to environmental responsibility is woven into every aspect of our operations:

  • Recycled Materials: 75% of our plastic content comes from post-consumer recycled materials
  • Energy Efficiency: Our facility runs on 60% renewable energy from solar panels installed in 2022
  • Waste Reduction: 98% of our manufacturing waste is recycled back into production
  • Sustainable Packaging: All packaging materials are recyclable or biodegradable
  • Carbon Neutral Shipping: We offset 100% of shipping emissions through verified carbon credit programs

We're proud to be certified as a B Corporation, meeting the highest standards of verified social and environmental performance, public transparency, and legal accountability.

title">Our Story

Founded in 2015 by industry veterans Michael Chen and Sarah Rodriguez, FlexiSeats Pro emerged from a simple vision: to create plastic chairs that combine durability, comfort, and environmental responsibility without compromising on style.

Starting in a modest 10,000 square foot facility in Springfield, Illinois, we've grown to become a trusted partner for restaurants, offices, event venues, and homes across the United States. Our commitment to quality and innovation has driven us to develop proprietary manufacturing processes that set new standards in the plastic furniture industry.

Today, we operate from our state-of-the-art 85,000 square foot manufacturing facility, producing over 500,000 chairs annually while maintaining our commitment to sustainable practices and exceptional customer service.
